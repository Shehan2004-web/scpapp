About the Calorie Calculator

Our Calorie Calculator estimates how many calories you need to consume daily to maintain, lose, or gain weight based on your age, gender, activity level, and goals. This is a useful tool for managing your nutrition and reaching your health and fitness goals.

Key Features

  • Accurate Calculation: Based on your weight, height, age, gender, and activity level.
  • Fitness Goals: Helps you calculate calories for weight maintenance, weight loss, or weight gain.
  • User-Friendly: A simple design that makes it easy for anyone to use.
  • 100% Free: No hidden fees or subscriptions; use the tool as often as you like.

How to Use the Calorie Calculator

  • Enter your age, gender, weight, height, and activity level.
  • Click the "Calculate Calories" button.
  • Your daily calorie needs will be displayed below, along with recommendations for maintaining, losing, or gaining weight.
