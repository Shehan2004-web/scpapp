About the Base64 Encoder/Decoder

Our Base64 Encoder/Decoder allows you to convert data into a Base64 encoded format and vice versa. This is useful for encoding binary data, text, and images into ASCII strings for transmission over text-based protocols.

Key Features

  • Fast Encoding and Decoding: Quickly convert your data to Base64 and back.
  • User-Friendly Interface: An intuitive design that makes it easy for anyone to use.
  • Supports Various Data Types: Encode and decode text, images, and other binary data types.
  • 100% Free: No hidden fees or subscriptions; use the tool as often as you like.

How to Use the Base64 Encoder/Decoder

  • Enter the text or upload the file you want to encode or decode.
  • Select the desired action: "Encode" or "Decode".
  • Click the "Submit" button to process your data.
  • Your encoded or decoded result will be displayed below.
